Iced Coffee

A refreshing iced coffee that can help you start your morning and keep you energized throughout the day

Learn how to make iced coffee with this iced coffee recipe! We use instant coffee in this cheater-version of iced cappuccino that's very easy to make, and very nice to drink! You are free to use the coffee granule of your liking.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Steps

  1. Combine warm water, instant coffee, and sugar in a sealable jar. Seal and shake until foamy.
  2. Pour into a glass full of ice; add milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.`
